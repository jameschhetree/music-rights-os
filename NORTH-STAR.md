> The all-in-one operating system for independent music business.
> Own your music. Maximize your income. Protect your rights.

Last updated: 2026-07-03

## What It Is

Music Rights OS is a cloud-based SaaS platform that helps independent artists, producers, and songwriters manage rights, registrations, royalties, contracts, and business operations in one place.

Artists lose thousands in royalties every year due to missed registrations, unclear ownership, poor organization, and lack of business knowledge. Music Rights OS gives them clarity, structure, and tools to protect their work and grow their income.

## Target Market

- **Primary users:** Independent artists, producers, songwriters, small label managers
- **Age range:** 16-45 with disposable income
- **Total addressable market:** ~10M+ independent artists globally (Spotify alone hosts 11M artists, ~95% independent)
- **Global recorded music industry (2024):** $29.6B (IFPI Global Music Report 2025)
- **Serviceable obtainable market (3-year target):** 10K-50K paying users (realistic), 500K is a 5+ year stretch goal

## The Problem

1. Artists lose royalties due to missing registrations
2. No centralized place to track ownership, splits, and rights
3. Confusing and fragmented music rights system
4. Manual processes lead to errors, missed opportunities, and lost money
5. Existing tools solve one piece (distribution OR publishing OR splits) but never all three

## Core Solution - 10 Modules

1. **Music Rights Dashboard** - Track all registrations, ownership percentages, and rights status in one view
2. **Split Sheet Generator** - Create professional split sheets with percentages, roles, and digital signatures
3. **Producer CRM** - Manage your contacts: artists, labels, managers, A&R, collaborators
4. **Release Calendar** - Automated content calendar to promote your releases
5. **Royalty Pocket** - Search your music across platforms and track royalties and distributions
6. **Copyright Checker** - Check for sample clearance, existing masters, and legal conflicts
7. **Music Business OS** - All your business operations: expenses, invoices, tax prep
8. **Opportunity Feed** - Daily updates on sync placements, contests, playlists, and opportunities
9. **License Generator** - Generate custom contracts and licensing terms
10. **AI Release Calendar** - Automated scheduling to promote your releases across platforms

## SWOT Analysis

**Strengths:**
- All-in-one platform (no one else does this)
- Low operating costs (cloud-based SaaS)
- Subscription revenue model = predictable MRR
- AI and automation make it scalable
- Addresses real pain (lost royalties, unclear ownership)

**Weaknesses:**
- New brand, no track record yet
- Competing against established names with millions of users
- Requires education - many artists don't know they need this
- Building trust in an industry full of scams

**Opportunities:**
- Independent artist market growing 20%+ YoY
- AI automation reduces education and management costs
- Expansion into adjacent markets (film, TV, gaming)
- Partnership with PROs, distributors, DAWs
- International expansion (music is global)

**Threats:**
- DistroKid or TuneCore adds rights management features
- Regulatory changes in music copyright law
- Slow adoption - artists are notoriously late adopters of business tools
- Larger players (Spotify, Apple) build native tools
